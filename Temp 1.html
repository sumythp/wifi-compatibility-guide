Quick Reference Guide

For Maximum Compatibility

AX200 Wi-Fi 6 Card: Works with 95% of systems from 2016+

AX210 Wi-Fi 6 Card: Works with 80% of systems from 2018+

BE200 Wi-Fi 7 Card: Works only with 2020+ systems

For Older Systems (Pre-2018)

9260 Wi-Fi 5 Card: Best for 2014-2017 systems

8265 Wi-Fi 5 Card: Good for 2016-2017 systems

7265 Wi-Fi 5 Card: Compatible with older systems

For Lenovo Systems

Look for FRU numbers: Essential for ThinkCentre models

Avoid generic cards: May not work due to BIOS whitelisting

Consider PCIe adapters: Can bypass whitelist restrictions

For HP/Dell Systems

More permissive: Generally accept standard Wi-Fi cards

Check generation: Newer models support more standards

No whitelist issues: Can use any compatible card

For Workstations

Dell Precision: Excellent compatibility, supports latest standards

Lenovo ThinkStation: Good compatibility, newer models support Wi-Fi 6/6E

HP Z Series: Best compatibility, supports full range of Wi-Fi standards

Installation Tips

Physical slot type: Check Key A, Key E, or Key A+E

Antenna cables: Ensure proper connection

Driver updates: Always update drivers after installation

BIOS updates: Update BIOS for better compatibility
